STEP 1 of understanding a DC motor

This model shows the magnetic field between the magnets and the current flowing through the wire when a battery is connected. It also shows the magnetic relationship between the wire and the magnets. STEP 3 of understanding a DC motor

This model shows the exploded view of a DC motor. It shows how the electric current flows through the coil and produces a torque that turns the DC motor, which is caused by the magnetic force. It shows the rotational force of the rotating armature, brush, and shaft.
